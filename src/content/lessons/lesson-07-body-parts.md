Knowing body parts is useful for medical situations or describing people.

## Head & Face

| English | Mara |
| :--- | :--- |
| Head | **Lu** |
| Hair | **Sami** |
| Eye | **Mo** |
| Nose | **Phanku** |
| Mouth | **Paka** |
| Ear | **Naho** |
| Tooth | **Hâ** |

## Body & Limbs

| English | Mara |
| :--- | :--- |
| Hand/Arm | **Ku** |
| Leg/Foot | **Phei / Pheilâ** |
| Stomach | **Chhutu** |
| Back | **Khnang** |
| Chest | **Kih** |

## Health Phrases

*   **Ei lu a pasâ.** (My head hurts / I have a headache.)
    *   *Lu* (Head) + *Pasâ* (Pain/Sick).
*   **Ei chhutu a pasâ.** (My stomach hurts.)
*   **Ei ku a khaw.** (My hand is broken/injured.)

## Practice

1. Touch your head and say "**Lu**".
2. Touch your ear and say "**Naho**".
3. Mime a headache and say "**Ei lu a pasâ.**"
